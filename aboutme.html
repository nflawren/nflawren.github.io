About Me

Hello! My name is Nicole Lawrence. I'm currently a full-time software engineer at The Walt Disney Company and a part-time graduate student at Boston University. I completed my Bachelor of Science at the University of Vermont, studying Computer Science in May 2021. I then moved to the Buffalo, NY area for my first job working as a software developer at M&T Bank. Then in mid-2022 I moved to the Bristol, CT area for my current position as a software engineer for The Walt Disney Company, where I work on ESPN.com.

I am originally from Jericho, VT, which is right outside of Burlington, VT. As a Vermont native, I love nature and being outside. Vermont has some beautiful state parks, and the lack of billboards really makes a difference in the everyday beauty! One of my favorite places is Lake Groton, VT, some of my favorite family vacations as a kid were trips there.
